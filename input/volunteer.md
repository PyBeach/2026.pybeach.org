PyBeach is a volunteer-run event, which means we rely exclusively on members of the community to offer their time and talents to make things happen!

## Interested in Helping out?

If you would like to help out, please fill out our [volunteer interest form](https://forms.gle/WWKu2YMZyisFm1og8). You can also reach out at [volunteer@pybeach.org](mailto:volunteer@pybeach.org) directly. Please also reach out if you have any other ideas for volunteer positions that might help make the conference better. Note: we will **not** be automatically providing free tickets to volunteers; instead you are encouraged to request financial assistance via [financialaid@pybeach.org](mailto:financialaid@pybeach.org).

## Volunteer Listings

Here are the things we currently need volunteer help with. This list will change over time as we get closer to the event date.

### Talk Reviewers {#review}

Help shape our conference program by providing feedback on our proposals. The CFP closes June 7th, and review period will last for roughly 2 months after that. Note that this requires relatively significant time commitment, please only volunteer if you can commit at least 2-4 hours per week during that time period.

### Outreach {#outreach}

We would like help reaching out to students, local companies, local user groups, and persons underrepresented in tech to get the word out to attend and submit talks.
