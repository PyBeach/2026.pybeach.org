## **Location** {#location}

Our venue is the [Illusion Magic Lounge](https://illusionmagiclounge.com/) located at 1418 4th St in the heart of downtown Santa Monica, California. It is a small 150-person performing arts space next to the historic 3rd street promenade, and 4 blocks away from the world-famous Santa Monica beach and Santa Monica Pier.

### **Arriving by Car** {#car}

The venue is conveniently located just off of the 4th street exit (exit 1A) on westbound Highway I-10. There are ample nearby [parking structures in downtown Santa Monica](https://www.santamonica.gov/places/parking-lots) which typically charge between $20-25 per weekend day  to park. The closest lot is [Structure 5](https://www.santamonica.gov/places/parking-lots/parking-structure-5).

### **Arriving by Transit** {#transit}

The venue is a short 7-minute walk from the Downtown Santa Monica station on the Metro E Line, or via the Big Blue Bus Rte \#1 along Santa Monica Blvd. Virtual fare cards can be purchased via Apple Wallet or via the [Transit](https://www.bigbluebus.com/Rider-Info/Track-Your-Ride-in-Real-Time!.aspx) and [TAP](https://www.taptogo.net/articles/en_US/Website_content/TAP-App) apps. Physical cards may be purchased from [metro station kiosks and retail partners](https://www.metro.net/riding/fares/#where). Cash is also accepted, but both systems require exact change. Further information on fares available here:

* [Big Blue Bus](https://www.bigbluebus.com/Newsroom/News/Big-Blue-Bus-implements-fare-policy-changes.aspx?type=News)
* [LA Metro](https://www.metro.net/riding/fares/)

### **Arriving by Small Personal Aircraft** {#plane}
Santa Monica Municipal Airport (SMO) is 4 miles from the venue and available to aircraft under 60,000 lbs. For information, visit the [airport's informational page](https://www.santamonica.gov/topic-explainers/aircraft-pilots).

## **Food/Drink** {#foodanddrink}

We plan to provide some small refreshments during breaks, however, to keep our costs low, meals will *not* be provided at this event. There are many great restaurants and cafes within a short walk of the venue. We've compiled a (non-exhaustive) list of recommendations [here](https://maps.app.goo.gl/HF6w4U6sS9499pXH8).

At the conclusion of the day on Saturday, our venue hosts will open their lobby lounge and bar for attendees to relax and hang out if they so choose. Note that while the conference itself is welcome to all ages, the lounge will be serving alcoholic drinks after the event.
